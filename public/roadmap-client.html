Platform Roadmap · Strategic Pillars
Where viax is headed — and what it means for your business.
Four interconnected investments define the viax platform direction for the next 12–18 months. Each pillar is designed to make it faster to build, simpler to operate, and more powerful to extend — without increasing the burden on your team.
viax
Roadmap · 2025–2026
Pillar 01
Spec-Driven Execution
Build new capabilities from natural language — not tickets.
  • Natural language modeling: Describe a process or rule in plain language. viax builds it.
  • Reusable skills library: Build once. Reuse across every new initiative without rebuilding.
  • Rapid new vertical prototyping: New channels and verticals live in days, not quarters.
  • UI generation from specs: Specs generate UI components automatically, cutting design-to-build time.
What this means for you: new revenue channels and business models move from concept to live in weeks, not quarters — without proportional growth in development overhead.
Pillar 02
Agentic Execution
Automate the lifecycle tasks that currently require manual intervention.
  • Scheduled task automation: Renewals, updates, and reports run automatically on schedule.
  • Event-triggered workflows: Agents activate on real events, reducing response time to seconds.
  • Agent invocation across the lifecycle: Execute agent tasks in defined workflow actions
  • Agent hosting on viax infrastructure: viax hosts and governs agents. No separate tooling required.
What this means for you: Define how agents participate in your revenue motions.
Pillar 03
Go-to-Market Efficiency
Faster path from business need to working demonstration to production.
  • Improved demo & prototyping tools: Validate working demos before any engineering investment is committed.
  • Customer-facing accelerators: Preconfigured templates for portals, ordering, and subscriptions.
  • Reduced onboarding friction: Each new vertical builds faster than the last.
  • Better visibility into progress: Clear progress dashboards. Your team always knows where things stand.
What this means for you: the gap between "we need this" and "this is live and generating value" gets shorter with every release cycle.
Pillar 04
Platform & Infrastructure
A more stable, faster, and simpler platform to build on.
  • More frequent releases with less risk: Smaller, more frequent releases with lower risk and coordination cost.
  • API simplification: Cleaner APIs mean less integration overhead for your team.
  • Reduced operational overhead: Automation eliminates manual release and compatibility coordination overhead.
  • Foundation for AI and agent capabilities: Cleaner infrastructure makes spec-driven and agentic capabilities reliable.
What this means for you: the platform gets faster and more reliable without requiring more from your team — and the foundation for everything above gets stronger.
